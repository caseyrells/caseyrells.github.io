Learn about me

DJ / Producer

  • Location: Austin, TX, USA

Whether it's working the crowd in large-scale clubs, providing a thicc vibe at smaller venues, or keeping guests entertained at private events, Rells' diverse background keeps him comfortable in all professional situations. Since his early days of rocking house parties to more recent full-on stage productions with hundreds or even thousands in attendance, Rells has gained the knowledge and experience only awarded to those with years in the industry. Equally comfortable both on-stage and in the studio, his formal training in Recording Engineering and Music Production compliments his DJ skills nicely.

DJ Services

What I Do

Nightclubs

My repertoire has been carefully crafted and honed over the years to get the club poppin. From the clutch song selections to the myriad technical skills I bring to the tables, you want me at the helm of your dance floor.

Bars/Lounges

It's not always about flashing lights, bottle service, and VIPs. Sometimes in smaller, more intimate venues you just need those good vibes. I got you covered and then some.

Private Events

Smaller events for private occasions? I got it on lock. Reunion parties, ceremonial events, debuts, graduation balls, anniversary parties, art gallery shows. I'm with it.

Corporate Events

Business gatherings, grand openings, product launches, app releases, office parties, you name it.

Weddings

While not my main focus, I've done many weddings over the years and can bring a great experience for you and your guests. Feel free to contact me for a quote.

Freelance Work

Outside of spinning records I also produce music, design software, make websites (including this one), edit audio & video, take photos, and a multitude of other things. Have some questions? Wanna collab? Hit me up.
