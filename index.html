BIO

Learn about me

DJ / Producer

  • Location: Austin, TX, USA

Whether it's commanding crowds in large-scale clubs, providing thicc vibes at more intimate venues, or keeping guests entertained at private events, my diverse background keeps me comfortable in all professional situations. My homebase is currently in Austin, but I'm no stranger to crossing state lines to perform nationally or globally. Since my early days of rocking house parties to more recent full-on stage productions with hundreds or even thousands in attendance, I've gained knowledge and experience only awarded to those with years in the industry. Equally comfortable both on-stage and in the studio, my formal training in Recording Engineering and Music Production compliments my DJ experience nicely.

DJ Services

What I Do

Nightclubs

Where I feel most at home. My repertoire has been carefully crafted and honed over the years to get the club SLAPPING. From the best custom song selections to the myriad technical skills I bring, you want me at the helm of your dance floor.

Bars/Lounges

It's not always about slaying a huge dance floor with flashing lights, bottle service, and VIPs. Sometimes in smaller, more intimate venues you just need those thicccc vibes. I got you covered and then some.

Private Events

Smaller events for private occasions? I got you fam. Reunion parties, Ceremonial events, debuts, graduation balls, anniversary parties, art gallery shows. I'm with it.

Corporate Events

Business gatherings, grand openings, product launches, store openings, app releases, office holiday parties, you name it.

Weddings

While not my main focus, I've done many weddings over the years and can bring a great experience for you and your guests. Feel free to contact me for a quote.

Freelance Work

Outside of spinning records I also produce music, design software, make websites (including this one), edit audio & video, take photos, and a multitude of other things. Have some questions? Wanna collab? Hit me up.
